**Soru Shiti – Tokenomics**

**Token:** $SORU  
**Blockchain:** Solana  
**Launch:** Pump.fun (Fair Launch)

### Token Supply
- **Total Supply:** 1,000,000,000 $SORU (1 Billion)
- True fair launch — No team tokens, no pre-mint, no taxes

### Core Economic Rule (Anti-FUD)
**100% of all $SORU spent in the game is instantly burned** and automatically converted into **Kami Points**.

### Creator Rewards
- Property owners earn **passive SOL** while their buildings stand
- This is the main way players earn real money

### Token Velocity & Deflation
- Extremely high deflationary pressure (100% burn on every spend)
- The more active the game, the faster $SORU supply decreases
- Late-game ages become very expensive and prestigious

### Design Goals
- Maximum transparency (no treasury FUD)
- Strong deflation narrative
- Healthy passive income for builders
- High gameplay velocity
